How to Use SchedulePro?

1. Login / Register

Start by logging in with your credentials or registering a new account. This ensures your tasks are secure and only visible to you.

2. Dashboard Overview

After login, you'll be taken to the dashboard where you can view an overview of your tasks, including upcoming and completed ones.

3. Create a New Task

  1. Click on the "Create Task" button.
  2. Fill in task details like title, description, deadline, and priority.
  3. Click "Save" to add the task to your list.

4. View All Tasks

Go to the "My Tasks" section to see a list of all your tasks. You can filter or sort them if needed.

5. Update a Task

Click the "Edit" or "Update" button next to a task to change its information. Submit the form to save changes.

6. Delete a Task

Click the "Delete" button to remove a task permanently. A confirmation will be shown before deletion.

7. Logout

When finished, click the "Logout" link to exit your session securely.
